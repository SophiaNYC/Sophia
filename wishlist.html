Wishlist

A soft mix of romance, minimalism and a touch of luxury. These are the little things that make my world more beautiful – from timeless keepsakes to quiet experiences we can share together. Feel free to choose whatever speaks to you most.

Luxury & Keepsakes

Small treasures I’ll keep and remember you by.

  • Delicate gold or rose-gold necklace
  • Thin diamond or gemstone bracelet
  • Minimalist stud earrings (pearls / diamonds)
  • Elegant, understated watch
  • Silk scarf in deep green, champagne or black
  • Leather cardholder or mini wallet
  • Elegant sunglasses for city walks

Fragrance & Atmosphere

Scents and details that stay on the skin and in the room.

  • Sensual evening perfume (amber, vanilla, oud)
  • Soft daytime perfume, close to the skin
  • Travel-size fragrance set for my handbag
  • Scented candles (vanilla, sandalwood, amber)
  • Luxury room spray or diffuser
  • Cozy throw or cashmere blanket

Romantic gestures

Tender, thoughtful gifts that feel like a warm hug.

  • White or cream roses
  • Peonies or seasonal pastel flowers
  • Beautiful notebook or journal
  • High-quality pen set
  • Handwritten card or letter

Beauty & feminine care

Little rituals that keep me glowing and soft.

  • Spa-quality body oil or lotion
  • Luxurious bath salts, oils or foam
  • Facial masks or skincare sets
  • Classic red lipstick
  • Neutral eyeshadow palette
  • Highlighter or blush for a soft glow
  • Silk or satin slip dress
  • Matching lingerie sets (black, emerald, champagne)
  • Soft, elegant pajamas

Gift cards

Minimal, easy and always appreciated.

  • Beauty & skincare gift card

  • Bookstore or art supply gift card
  • Spa or wellness gift card

Experiences to share

Moments together are always the most precious.

  • Dinner at a cozy restaurant or wine bar
  • Jazz club or piano bar evening
  • Tickets to a play, ballet or concert
  • Museum or gallery visit
  • Short getaway or hotel stay
  • Rooftop drinks with a city view
  • Sunset walk with dessert afterwards

If you prefer, you’re always welcome to choose a simple gift card instead of a specific item. I’ll quietly turn it into something beautiful to remember you by.
